Additional costs:


Airport transfer
17,50.- Euro for the transfer from the airport (one way), total 35,- Euro (return ticket)
These prices are per taxi and will be divided by the number of passengers.
If you book the transfer with us, you will be picked up at the airport by our driver who will then bring you directly to our Villa Arana. The transfer takes about 30 minutes, depending on the traffic.
 
Visa
To enter Sri Lanka, a visa is usually required, depending on the citizenship. The tourist visa is valid for 30 days and can be extended in Colombo if you plan to stay in Sri Lanka longer.

There are two ways to apply for a visa:
- The visa can be applied for before the trip online at http://www.eta.gov.lk/slvisa/ (approx. 35 US dollars) or
- when entering the country at the "Visa on Arrival" counter (approx. 40 US dollars)
There you can also pay in Euro (approx. 40 Euro). This may take some time, depending on the number of people who require a visa when entering the country.

In both cases it is important that the passport is valid for at least 6 months on the date of departure.
 
Flights 
Our guests book the flights themselves according to their preferred flight times, through any travel agency or directly online. We have had good experiences with the Google flight search (www.google.de/flights) as well as the flight search engine www.skyscanner.com .
 
Currently the only international passenger airport in Sri Lanka is Bandaranaike Airport Colombo-Katunayake, abbreviation CMB.
